# Concept Crosswalks

<!-- updated at: 2026-07-07 21:15 Z   (2026-07-07 17:15 EDT) -->

One file per concept, each comparing all 8 source ontologies in scope (BFO, DOLCE, SUMO, UFO, GFO, YAMATO, TUpper, GUM — see [ADR-0015](../../adr/ADR-0015-expand-source-ontology-scope-to-eight.md)) against directly-verified primary sources. All 26 reviewed concepts — the original 8 plus all 9 of the `0.2.0` batch ([ADR-0018](../../adr/ADR-0018-comprehensive-concept-coverage-staged-via-minor-releases.md)) plus `disposition-capacity.md`, `mind-conscious-being-agent.md`, `ontological-level-stratum.md`, `change.md`, `list-sequence.md`, `continuous-discrete.md`, `modality.md`, `non-physical-social-object.md`, and `symbol-sign-representation.md` — are `reviewed`. Per [ADR-0021](../../adr/ADR-0021-source-classified-core-placement-criterion.md), all 26 qualify for XwkOnt's core module (`data/ontology/core.ttl`); the actual Turtle additions are separately-tracked work, not yet done. **`ontological-level-stratum.md` is the 20th `reviewed` concept**, the third reviewed concept of the `0.3.0` batch ([ADR-0018](../../adr/ADR-0018-comprehensive-concept-coverage-staged-via-minor-releases.md)'s single-source rung) — a single-source (GFO-only) concept once a Codex draft claim that UFO's UFO-A/UFO-B/UFO-C partition was a second source was independently checked and resolved against on direct evidence (the source paper never uses stratification vocabulary, and the partition's substance duplicates `continuant-occurrent.md`/`role.md` content already); this candidate was already admitted to the batch on single-source terms, so that finding confirmed rather than changed the batch's original scope. **`change.md` is the 21st `reviewed` concept**, the fourth reviewed concept of the `0.3.0` batch — a two-source (GFO, SUMO) concept scoped specifically around the extended-process reading of GFO `Change` left unmapped by `event.md`'s existing `Instantaneous_change` correspondence, plus SUMO's independently-reified `InternalChange` family (found via `candidate-concepts.md`'s own raw inventory, not the original `TODO.md` seed, which had missed it). **`list-sequence.md` is the 22nd `reviewed` concept**, the fifth reviewed concept of the `0.3.0` batch — a two-source (SUMO, GUM) concept where a Codex draft's single-source (SUMO-only) framing was corrected during independent verification: GUM's `OrderedObject`/`OrderedSet` was found directly against `GUM-31.owl`, a source the draft had not checked at all, and `candidate-concepts.md`'s own rows for those two classes were retagged from unrelated buckets (Mereology/Parthood/Aggregate, Universal/Type) to `List / Sequence`. **`continuous-discrete.md` is the 23rd `reviewed` concept**, the sixth reviewed concept of the `0.3.0` batch — a single-source (GFO-only) concept covering GFO's own reified `Continuous`/`Discrete` split over `Individual` (with `Discrete_presential` as the presential-side branch), scoped to avoid re-deriving axioms already cited in passing by `quantity-amount-of-matter.md`, `object.md`, and `continuant-occurrent.md`. Independent review found and corrected a citation defect in the Codex draft (a quote misattributed to SUMO that actually belongs to TUpper) and downgraded an unsubstantiated UFO re-access claim to an open verification gap, consistent with the same-day UFO access block `list-sequence.md`'s review hit. **`modality.md` is the 24th `reviewed` concept**, the seventh reviewed concept of the `0.3.0` batch — a single-source (GUM-only) concept covering GUM's own reified `ModalQuality` branch (`Conditional`/`NonConditional`/`NonVolitional`/`Volitional`/`Necessity`/`Possibility`) and its ascription relation `ModalPropertyAscription`, scoped to avoid re-deriving `quality.md`'s `SimpleQuality` finding and `disposition-capacity.md`'s `GeneralPossibility`/`Ability` findings. Independent review confirmed two of the Codex draft's UFO quotations against a genuine (if one-day-stale) full-paper extraction after this session's own live DOI/mirror fetch hit the same recurring HTTP 403 block `continuous-discrete.md`/`list-sequence.md` hit, and corrected one factual error (GUM's `PropertyAscription` superclass does not itself disjoin `ModalPropertyAscription` from its siblings — the correct axiom is declared reciprocally on `LogicalPropertyAscription`'s own block). **`non-physical-social-object.md` is the 25th `reviewed` concept**, the eighth reviewed concept of the `0.3.0` batch — a two-source (DOLCE, UFO) concept covering DOLCE's base-module `non-physical-endurant`/`non-physical-object` branch, scoped against three already-`reviewed` DOLCE-family crosswalks (`situation-state-of-affairs.md`, `role.md`, `information-artifact.md`) that touch adjacent DOLCE "social object" territory via different artifacts, none of which cite core `DOLCE-Lite.owl` itself for this material. Independent review found and corrected three citation-fidelity defects in the Codex draft (a truncated DOLCE quotation, a wrong BFO IRI, a SUMO paraphrase mislabeled as a direct quotation) and found a genuine second source the draft missed entirely: UFO's paper uses the identical phrase "non-physical endurants" informally, in a footnote with a worked example. **`symbol-sign-representation.md` is the 26th `reviewed` concept**, completing standalone-crosswalk work on all 10 of the `0.3.0` batch's originally-identified candidates — a two-source (GFO, GUM) concept covering the sign-vehicle/symbolization layer, narrowed in earlier sessions to exclude YAMATO's `representing thing` and SUMO's `SymbolicString`/`Icon`/`LinguisticExpression` as duplicative of `information-artifact.md`'s own already-cited evidence, while retaining GFO's `Symbol_structure` and GUM's `Symbolization`/`Signification`/`NameOf`/`NameRelation` since `information-artifact.md` itself considered and rejected them only for being the wrong *kind* (type-side category; relation-type), not as already-covered content. Independent re-verification against all 8 primary sources found and corrected two truncated GUM quotations (`Symbolization`, `Signification`) and restored a dropped second sentence on `NameEvent`; no other defects found. `core.ttl` placement for this and the `0.3.0` batch's other 8 newer `reviewed` concepts remains separately-tracked work, not yet done. New crosswalks follow [TEMPLATE.md](TEMPLATE.md) and the structure/status conventions in [docs/INFORMATION_ARCHITECTURE.md](../../INFORMATION_ARCHITECTURE.md) and [docs/methodology/crosswalk-methodology.md](../../methodology/crosswalk-methodology.md).
