## Scope Note

This crosswalk compares bearer-inherent capacities in the BFO sense: `disposition` and its etiological subtype `function`, against candidate analogs in GFO, UFO/OntoUML, SUMO, YAMATO, and GUM.

It does **not** compare Role or Relator. Those are social/relational positions or truthmakers, not intrinsic capacities. It also does not treat procedure, intention, or other modal-looking lexical neighbors as equivalent just because they sound ability-like.

Checked DOLCE materials (`D18`, `DOLCE-Lite`, and DUL) did not yield a confirmed bearer-capacity analogue in the source material read for this pass. That is recorded here as a scoped negative finding, not as a claim about every later DOLCE extension.
